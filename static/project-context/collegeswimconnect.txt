Tagline:
Built by D1 swimmers, backed by NCAA coaches — CollegeSwimConnect equips D1 prospects with proven strategies and personal guidance to navigate recruiting and reach their dream school.

High-level summary:
CJ served as Product Manager at CollegeSwimConnect, a recruiting and mentorship platform for D1 swimming prospects. CJ uncovered operational inefficiencies and positioned the platform for scalable growth. CJ owned end-to-end product strategy from user discovery through MVP delivery, collaborating with engineers and designers to streamline workflows, improve trust-building, align features with long-term platform goals, and deliver tangible outcomes.

The core problem:
CollegeSwimConnect had strong early traction but lacked scalability. Despite clear value, the platform struggled to grow its user base and deliver mentorship efficiently. The key question CJ focused on was: if the platform already had value, what was preventing growth?

CJ's role:
CJ led two Agile sprints — design and engineering — focused on expanding internal features to streamline operations. The final demo cut admin workload by 55% and mentor workload by 50%, freeing time for high-quality mentorship.

Key deliverables:

1. User Research
CJ conducted discovery research with D1 athletes and recruits to identify workflow pain points and understand where the platform was creating friction for both administrators and mentors.

2. Agile Sprints
CJ led two sprints — a design sprint and an engineering sprint — to move from discovery insights to a working MVP with measurable operational improvements.

3. Database and Backend
CJ managed the design of a relational schema in PostgreSQL, app deployment via Railway, and backend design in Python and Flask.

4. Impact
The work reduced admin workload by 55% and mentor workload by 50%, eliminating operational noise and freeing time for high-quality mentorship delivery.
